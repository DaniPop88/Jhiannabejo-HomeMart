Privacy Policy

At Jhiannabejo Home Mart, your privacy is our top priority. We believe in transparency and trust, and we are committed to protecting your personal information every step of the way. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you shop with us, both online and in-store.

What Information We Collect

  • Contact details (such as your name, phone number, email address, and delivery address)
  • Order and transaction history
  • Payment information (for processing your orders securely)
  • Feedback and communication records

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To get in touch and confirm your order details, product preferences, and delivery arrangements
  • To improve our products and services based on your feedback
  • To keep you updated about your orders, new products, and store promotions (only with your consent)

How We Protect Your Data

  • All personal data is stored securely and treated with confidentiality
  • Access to customer information is strictly limited to authorized staff
  • We use secure technologies to protect payment information

Your Rights & Choices

  • You can request access to your personal information at any time
  • You may update or correct your details by contacting our team
  • You can opt out of marketing communications whenever you wish
  • If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to reach out

Sharing Your Information

Jhiannabejo Home Mart does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties. We only share information when necessary to complete your order (such as with delivery partners) or when required by law.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on our website. If major changes are made, we will notify you directly.
