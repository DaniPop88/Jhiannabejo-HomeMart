About Jhiannabejo Home Mart

Jhiannabejo Home Mart started with a simple mission: to make quality home goods accessible to every Filipino family. Founded in 2020 by a local Quezon City entrepreneur, we began as a humble neighborhood tindahan and grew into a full-fledged home mart through the trust of our community.

We proudly serve Barangay Central and surrounding areas, providing practical items for modern Filipino homes—whether you're a student living in a dorm, a young couple starting a family, or a homemaker looking for everyday value.

  • Tiwala (Trust): We only sell items we'd use in our own homes.
  • Sulit (Value for money): Affordability with no compromise on quality.
  • Alaga (Care): Personalized service that treats every customer like family.
